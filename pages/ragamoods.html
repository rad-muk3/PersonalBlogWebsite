Did you know that Ragaas or Raagams in addition to giving a musical trend is also a mood setter? Ragas evoke sadness, romance, peace, strength/courage, anger, joy and devotion. Let's see a few Ragaas and what moods or emotions they capture? Shall we!

Anandha Bhairavi

  • "Anandam" means "happiness" and hence this raga brings about a mood of Happiness
  • It spreads the feeling of joy and fulfillment
  • Its gliding nature of scales is so mystical, it brings devotion and compassion

Malahari

  • It is a beginners Raga and bring about a mood of Calmness
  • It is known as a Morning Raga
  • This is an ancient Ragam , which legend tells has brought forth Rains

Dharmavati

  • It brings sweetness
  • It is a romantic ragam, based on eternity and love
  • It feels so soothing like honey

Hamsadhwani

  • Brings forth positive vibe and energy
  • Brings forth a sense of well-being
  • Certainly a happy Ragam
